Design Principle Sheet

Color

It is important that the color you decide to use in your page has an overall easy color scheme that isn't overwhelming. When you are using more than 5 color on your page it can have an overwhelming effect that will confuse your visitors. There are certain color schemes that should be practiced to give your page an aesthetically pleasing design (Analogous, Complementary, Triadic, Quadratic).

If you use the correct colors your page will be able to efficiently communicate the purpose of your site. If not, users can become confused and will often leave the site with no intention to return.
